FAQ
Why does not gspeakers display any text in the plot? I also get a lot of error messages in the console.
You probably have gspeakers configured to use a font which does not exist on your system. Open preferences dialog and select a font.
GSpeakers crashes upon startup, what is wrong?
There might be an error in the config-file. Try "mv ~/.gspeakers ~/.gspeakers.bak" and restart gspeakers. There might also be a problem if you specified a "pkgdatadir" different from "[prefix]/share/gspeakers" (where [prefix] is the prefix specified when you ran ./configure).
